# Roadmap

High-level direction for Dividend Tracker. Tracked as issues; this is the
curated view. Suggestions welcome via
[feature requests](https://github.com/leviceroy/Dividend-Tracker/issues/new?template=feature_request.yml).

## Shipped

- Multi-account / multi-currency income ledger with reporting-currency conversion
- Live prices → market value, current yield, unrealized / realized P&L, total return
- Forward income (TTM DPS), yield-on-cost on capital actually invested
- Dividend CAGR, growth streaks, cut detection
- Withholding-tax tracking + Swiss DA-1 reclaim
- Stock splits, special dividends, DRIP reinvestment
- FIRE / income-target progress
- In-app auto-update

## Planned

- Expense ratio & TTM yield per instrument
- Real declared ex-dividend dates (vs. the current history-based projection)
- DA-1 tax-year CSV export
- Benchmark vs. an index
- Windows build

## Considering

- Subscription licensing (the app will remain usable; premium features TBD)
